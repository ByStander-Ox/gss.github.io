"Horizontally center the devices, & the bottom of the iPhone & iPad should be equal."
With GSS, the syntax is true to our intentions: {% highlight css %} #iphone[center-x] == #ipad[center-x]; #iphone[bottom] == #ipad[bottom]; {% endhighlight %}
